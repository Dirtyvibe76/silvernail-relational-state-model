# SRSM Paper 2 — Induced Metric Dynamics and Quadratic Gauge Closure

This directory supports Paper 2 of the Silvernail Relational State Model research program.

## Scope

- V0.74: common-frame universality and conditional induced metric dynamics;
- V0.76: exact quadratic lattice gauge-Hessian closure;
- V0.77: rejection of the naive nonlinear finite-difference completion;
- V0.79: constrained tetrad–connection prototype and torsion elimination.

## Scientific boundary

The results establish a conditional effective-field-theory route to an Einstein–Hilbert term and an exact quadratic gauge sector with two transverse-traceless tensor modes. They do not establish exact nonlinear finite-spacing constraint closure, calculate Newton's constant or the cosmological constant, or complete a microscopic quantum-gravity theory.

See `records/`, `data/verified_values.csv`, and `scripts/generate_paper2_figures.py`.
