# Paper 1 submission snapshot

Target journal: *Foundations of Physics*.

The manuscript is prepared as an original research article. Its claims are deliberately restricted to:

- exact algebraic properties of the declared finite generators;
- finite-system commutator-propagation diagnostics;
- delayed-injection discrimination among candidate relational metric histories;
- the incidence-generator, graph Klein–Gordon, infrared Dirac-type, and Clifford-closure constructions under their stated assumptions.

The manuscript expressly excludes claims of exact Lorentz invariance, a thermodynamic-limit theorem, or a completed derivation of general relativity.

## Files for submission

- Manuscript: Google Docs master, exported as DOCX or PDF at submission time.
- Figures: five verified computational figures generated by `submission/make_verified_figures.py`.
- Code and data: this public repository.
- Cover letter: maintained with the manuscript submission package.
- Declarations: funding, competing interests, author contributions, and data/code availability are included in the manuscript.

## Version policy

The journal submission must cite the exact Git commit used. Any later revisions should be tagged separately so that the evidence underlying the submitted manuscript remains identifiable.
